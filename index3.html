ROHIT SARAF-AUTOBIOGRAPHY

He is a famous TV actor, who apart from Television, has won the hearts of the
audience with his strong acting in many web series.
He was born on 8 December 1996 in Kathmandu, Nepal. He started his career
as a model, for which he shifted from Delhi to Mumbai.
His full name is Rohit Suresh Saraf, but he is famous among the audience as
Rohit Saraf.

ROHIT SARAF-ACTING CAREER

Rohit Suresh Saraf started his acting career with TV Shows. Along with this, he
made his film debut with a supporting role in Dear Zindagi which was released
in 2016.

He has worked in the Norwegian film What Will People Do, the comedy-drama
Rani Mukharji Starrer Hichki (2018), the biopic The Sky Is Pink (2019) alongside
Priyanka Chopra and Zaira Wasim, the dark comedy Ludo (2020), and the thriller
Hrithik Roshan starrer Vikram Vedha (2022). He has also done the Netflix series
Mismatched (2020)

His first lead film role was in the romantic comedy Ishq Vishk Rebound (2024)
which was well-received by fans. In 2018, he starred in opposite YRF Comedy
-drama Film Hichki. He also appeared alongside an ensemble cast in Anurag
Basu’s black comedy Ludo.

He played a leading role opposite to Anandi in the Tamil romantic drama Kamili
From Nadukkaveri and starred as Hrithik Roshan’s younger brother in Vikram
Vedha. He also appeared in Mani Ratnam’s Tamil film Thug Life.

Now, he will soon be seen in the Netflix Romantic Comedy Series Mismatched
Season 3. As per media reports, he will be seen in Dharma Productions romantic
comedy Sunny Sanskari Ki Tulsi Kumari (2025) starring Varun Dhawan, Janhvi
Kapoor,and Sanya Malhotra.

Rohit-AWARDS AND NOMINATIONS

Rohit Saraf has received many awards and nominations in his acting career. His acting style
and charming personality have given him a place in the audience’s hearts. He was ranked
at No. 39 in The Times Most Desirable Men in 2020.
